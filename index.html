Jowis Travel, based in Limpopo, South Africa, offers comprehensive travel consulting for both local and international clients, specializing in corporate and leisure travel. We prioritize building strong client and supplier relationships, focusing on reliability, flexibility, and affordability. Collaborating with experienced Travel Management Companies worldwide, we negotiate the best rates for our clients. Our services include flights, airport transfers, accommodation, car hire, private charters, visa applications, and travel insurance. With a 24-hour emergency service, we ensure prompt handling of booking changes. We also specialize in organizing conferences, events, and group travels, guaranteeing seamless coordination and exceptional experiences.
